What kind of mobile phone repairs do we carry out?

We can fix most mobile phone faults and problems, including:

  • Warranty defects
  • Screen replacement, e.g. if your screen or digitizer has smashed/stopped responding
  • Water damage, e.g. if you’ve dropped or splashed your phone in liquid, however most water damage is beyond repair
  • Broken casings, e.g. if you’ve dropped your phone
  • Non-working handsets, e.g. battery or charging issues
  • Software fixes, e.g. upgrades and factory resets
  • Hardware repairs, e.g. speaker/microphone repairs and keypad problems.

We’ll do everything we can to get your mobile phone back into your hands as quickly as possible.

How can you get a quote and repair?

The Fastest way to get your device fixed is to make a reservation at our office. Our repair centre have sophisticated onsite repair facilities. As long as we have all the parts required for your repair in stock, we aim to get your device back to you in a short time.

If your hardware is fine yet there is problem with your device, we can help you fix the software issues in no time.
