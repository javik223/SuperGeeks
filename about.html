About

SuperGeeks Nigeria SuperGeeks is an after sales device repair and service chain, and the leading providers of after sales technical support to customers within the Lagos metropolis, with focus on customer service.

 

In the beginning, we longed for an environment, where technical support, repairs and maintenance of consumer electronics were simple, hassle and stress free, and most importantly, done with expertise and attention to detail, but it just wasn’t existing at the time.

We set out to create a solution that was not only simple, hassle and stress free, but comes with a great customer experience.

We know our client use their devices and electronics for work and leisure, and place so much importance on the effectiveness and efficiency of those devices. We focus on providing after sales and technical support to our clients to maintain their devices working at 100%.

Our principle hinges on quality service delivery and customer satisfaction. Our strength lies in our expertise and know-how in fixing, servicing and maintaining a wide range of customer electronics.

Our people are called Geeks; Super ones actually, because of their expertise and know-how and they always take proper care of their own (Your devices). Making your devices and the IT infrastructures you love so much working at 100% is what we always aim for. This is the oath we’ve taken and it’s what we live by.
